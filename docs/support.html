Getting started

1. Open Screenshot Buddy. It lives in your menu bar, top-right of the screen.
2. Click Connect Your Screenshots Folder and choose where your screenshots are saved (usually your Desktop, unless you've changed it).
3. Click the menu bar icon any time to see your screenshots.

Where do my screenshots save by default?

On most Macs, screenshots land on your Desktop. You can change this in the macOS Screenshot app (press Shift-Command-5, then Options).

What's the difference between "Sweep to Trash" and "Delete Forever"?

Sweep to Trash moves everything to the macOS Trash, so you can recover files the normal way. Delete Forever removes them permanently to free the space immediately; right after, you get a few seconds to click Undo if you change your mind.

How do I change the folder?

Click the menu bar icon, then the gear, then Change Folder…

Does Screenshot Buddy send my screenshots anywhere?

Never. Everything happens on your Mac. See our Privacy Policy.
