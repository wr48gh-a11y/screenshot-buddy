# Screenshot Buddy Privacy Policy

_Last updated: July 18, 2026_

Screenshot Buddy is designed to protect your privacy completely.

## What we collect
Nothing. Screenshot Buddy does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party services.

## What the app accesses
Screenshot Buddy accesses only the single folder you explicitly choose, and only to display, open, rename, or delete the screenshots inside it. This all happens locally on your Mac. Your files never leave your computer.

## Data storage
The only thing Screenshot Buddy saves is a secure reference (a security-scoped bookmark) to the folder you selected, stored locally on your Mac so it can remember your choice between launches. This never leaves your device.

## Children's privacy
Screenshot Buddy does not collect data from anyone, including children.
